Brand With Us

Branded paper cups are the perfect opportunity to get your message to your consumer; whether it is a brand marketing campaign, a seasonal message, or a promotional program, custom printed cups are certain way to make your business stand out. In order to stand out in a now saturated market, it is important to be different, and in order to do this, you need to be recognizable. Branded cups are now becoming an integral part of brand positioning for coffee houses to maximize visibility on the high street.



The process of generating your own branded paper cups

ARTWORK GENERATER

Here is when you send your design,or you use our in house studio to create a branded paper cup for your business

ARTWORK & COLOUR SIGN OFF

To make sure your chosen design and colours appear exactly as planned, we do a draft print for authorisation prior to production.

PRINTING

Now you're are happy with your design it is time to put your cubs into production,using our state of the art printer which benefits from environmentally considerate water-based inks.

PAPER CUP FORMATION

Once the design is printed,now the cups can be formed using our high speed machines that manufacture more than 2.5 million paper cups per day.

DELIVERY

Your branded paper cups are ready to got from here our warehouse will distribute your paper cups to a place of your choice

BRANDS IN HANDS

The final and most important part,your branded cup reaches it's final destination,placing your message into the hands of your consumer

Paper Cups For Your Business

Reassuring our customers with our environmental commitment all of Sprout paper cups are sourced from sustainably managed forestry and their paper board is all PEFC certified. Our cups are water coated with water base emulsion certified and approved by the Government of Kerala. Our cups are 100% biodegradable, recyclable and compostable. Not only do we focus on our responsibility for our raw materials, but we have consciously decided to use only water based inks during the printing of our branded paper cups, as well as our stock design, this alternative to acrylic based inks means that our printing process is much more environmentally considerate.
